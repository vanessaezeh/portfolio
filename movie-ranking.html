The Best Movie According to Vanessa

My top 3 movie of all-times

Boys Before Flower

This is the first korean drama i watched. It was a romance themed drama. I loved the high school romance that took place in the drama.

Descendants of the Sun

It is also a korean drama. I am a very big fan of korean drama. This is also a romance drama between a doctor and a solider as they worked to saved people that were involved in an Earthquake incident. I loved the character the doctor portraryed as she tried to save as much lives as she could.

Vincenzo

Its an action and romance themed movie of a well known italian korean mafia lawyer who came back to korean for some business. I really loved the actions in the movie.
